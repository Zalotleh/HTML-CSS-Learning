British Shorthair

About the Breed

British Shorthairs are a breed of cat that originated in the United Kingdom.

History

British Shorthairs were first bred in the United Kingdom in the early 1970s.

Behavior

British Shorthairs are very social and friendly cats. They are very active and enjoy being around other cats.

Health

British Shorthairs are very healthy and are very well suited for indoor environments.

Temperament

British Shorthairs are very calm and quiet cats. They are very gentle and are very affectionate.
